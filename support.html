Frequently asked

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Open Settings on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Found → Cancel. You'll keep access until the end of the current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

Inside the Found app, go to Settings → Delete Account. Your profile, photos, and messages will be removed within 30 days. Some data may be retained where required by law.

I think I was matched with a bot or fake profile

Tap the profile → Report → select a reason. Our trust & safety team reviews reports within 24 hours. You can also email hello@foundmatchmaker.com with details.

How does Found keep me safe?

We verify profile photos with a selfie check, use AI to detect bots and scams, limit how fast strangers can escalate conversations, and remove anyone who violates our rules. However, no app can guarantee safety — always meet in public, tell a friend, and trust your instincts. See our Terms of Service for the full picture.

Why hasn't my match replied?

Everyone's on their own timeline. Some people check in once a day, others less. Give it space. If days pass, Found will gently surface someone new.

Is Found only in Australia?

Right now we're launching in Manly, Sydney — but the app works anywhere in the world. More cities coming soon.
